I Instrumental No Vocals
J Jazzy Jazz tinged feel or influences to a song that isn’t pure or standard Jazz.
K Alt Country eg 1
Alt Country eg 2
Alt Country eg 3 		Not pure country music = less ‘twangy’ singing, perhaps electronic or punk sounds, perhaps lyrical content is less about traditional country themes (wife left, dog died etc), and more about other themes
k Soul
L Live A live performance version of a song
M Metal Classic heavy metal rock & rock = Old school includes Metallica, Megadeath
New School = Dragonforce, The Darkness, Limp Bizkit (with SC Rap & Hip Hop)
N Indie Not used to describe a specific ‘sound’ or music genre. Indie stands for Independent artist ‘unsigned’ to a major record label.  Helps Edge Dj's spot an artist that is potentially underrepresented in mainstream commercial media
O World eg 1
World eg 2
World eg 3 		Non-English language vocals, or music clearly influenced by other nationalities or cultures. Good world music for Edge is often a 'mash up' of genre's & styles
o Atmospheric Soundscape, limited percussion or drum beats
P Punk eg 1
Punk eg 2 		Includes old school punk and garage, and new school including emo
p Progressive Tricky one. Use for a combination with another Eg ‘Prog Rock’ is Category=Rock SC=Progressive Progressive house music would be Cat=Electronic SC=Progressive & House
Q Trance Solid dance beat with mix of euphoric highs and spacey bridges
q Roots For Edge it = Smooth ‘sensitive surfy’ style music. Jack Johnson, beautiful girls, Xavier Rudd etc
R Retro Denotes a time eg Anything Pre 1990's era, but can also include new music that has a 'classic era' feel (eg Wolfmother have a retro 1970's rock feel)
r Reggae
Reggae with Rap 		Guitars on the upbeat, smooth beats and basslines. Includes dub Cat = Rock SC = Reggae and Rap (for spoken work lyrics)
S Ska eg 1
Ska eg 2 		Sounds like reggae sped up with Punk influences. Often verses have the reggae 1&3 beat syncopation and the choruses break into a straight punk rock style
t Techno
U Unplugged An acoustic or stripped back version of a song
v Cover Version
W Rap New School
Rap Old School 		Spoken word style vocal deliver
w Rocky Use in combination with non Rock category song Eg a house music song with guitar samples could be Cat = Electronic SC= House & Rocky
X Hardcore Very heavy metal, often with deep grinding vocals
x Tekky Beeps & squeaks and mixed up song structure
Y Film Related From a soundtrack or well known as featured in a movie
y Britpop Pommy accents and poppy guitars - oasis, blur supergrass
z Industrial Rocky Industrial House Heavy, possibly electronic and distorted aggressive feel. Use in combination with categories * other sound codes Eg Nine Inch Nails is Cat = Rock SC = Industrial A Rammstein dance remix may be Cat= Electronic SC= Rocky & Industrial
